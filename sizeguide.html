Chest Measurement

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape level under your arms.

Waist Measurement

Measure around your natural waistline, keeping the tape comfortably loose.

Hip Measurement

Measure around the fullest part of your hips, about 8 inches below your waist.

Inseam Measurement

Measure from the crotch to the bottom of your ankle along the inside of your leg.

Foot Measurement

Measure your foot from heel to toe while standing. Do this for both feet.

Measurement Instructions

  • Use a soft, flexible measuring tape for accuracy.
  • Measure over light clothing or undergarments.
  • Keep the tape parallel to the floor and snug but not tight.
  • For best results, have someone help you with measurements.
  • Measure twice to ensure accuracy.
  • Stand naturally with your weight evenly distributed.
  • Refer to our size charts for specific garment measurements.
