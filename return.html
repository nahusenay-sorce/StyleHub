Returns & Refunds — Easy, Fast, Fair

At Vanguard, your satisfaction is our priority. We've designed our return policy to be as straightforward and hassle-free as possible. If you're not completely happy with your purchase, we'll make it right.

Returns Policy

Please do not send your return to the manufacturer. Follow our return process below.

Eligible Items

  • Unused items in original condition
  • With all tags attached
  • Within 14 days of delivery
  • In original packaging

Non-Eligible Items

  • Underwear & swimwear (for hygiene reasons)
  • Final sale items (marked at checkout)
  • Items damaged by customer
  • Personalized or custom-made products

Shipping Instructions

  • Use original packaging when possible
  • Include your order number inside the package
  • Customer pays return shipping unless it's our error
  • Ship to: Vanguard Returns, 123 Fashion Ave, New York, NY 10001

How to Initiate a Return

  1. Log in to your Vanguard account
  2. Go to "Order History"
  3. Select the item(s) you wish to return
  4. Print the prepaid return label (if eligible)
  5. Package your item securely
  6. Drop off at any authorized shipping center
Start Return Process

Refunds Policy

Refund Timeline

  • Processed within 1-2 business days after we receive your return
  • Issued to your original payment method
  • 5-7 business days for bank processing
  • Email notification sent when refund is processed

Refund Methods

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Refunded to original card
  • PayPal: Refunded to your PayPal account
  • Store Credit: Available immediately as e-voucher
  • Bank Transfer: For international returns (may take 10-14 days)

Special Cases

  • Defective items: Full refund without return
  • Wrong item shipped: Free return shipping + refund
  • Damaged items: Partial refund based on condition
  • Lost in return shipping: Contact us with tracking info

Refunds may take up to 10 business days to appear in your account depending on your bank's processing time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I return a product I wore once?

We accept returns on gently worn items only if they show no signs of wear and all tags are still attached. Final discretion rests with our quality control team. Items showing significant wear cannot be returned.

Will I be refunded for the delivery cost?

Original delivery costs are non-refundable except in cases where we made an error (wrong item shipped, defective product, etc.). Return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility unless the return is due to our error.

What if I received the wrong item?

If you received an incorrect item, we apologize for the inconvenience. Contact us within 7 days of delivery and we'll email you a prepaid return label. Once we receive the incorrect item, we'll ship the correct item immediately at no cost to you.

Can I exchange instead of returning?

Yes! We offer free size exchanges within 30 days of purchase. Initiate an exchange through your account dashboard. We'll ship the new size immediately and provide a prepaid label to return the original item. Exchanges are only available for the same product in a different size.

What if my return is lost in transit?

We recommend using a tracked shipping method. If your return shipment gets lost, we can only process your refund after we receive the item. If you used our prepaid label, we'll file a claim with the carrier. For self-shipped returns, please file a claim with your shipping provider.
