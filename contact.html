Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions. For more details, visit our full FAQ page.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within Ethiopia. For international orders, delivery may take 7-14 business days depending on your location.

Go to Shipping FAQ
Can I return a sale item?

Yes, sale items can be returned within 14 days of purchase, provided they are in their original condition with tags attached. Final sale items are marked as non-returnable during checkout.

View Return Policy
How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website.

Track Your Order
What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and mobile money services. We also offer cash on delivery for local orders.

Payment Options
Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. Duties and taxes may apply depending on your country's regulations.
