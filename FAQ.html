Shipping & Delivery

What countries do you ship to?

We currently ship to over 100 countries worldwide, including all major markets in North America, Europe, Asia, and Australia. During checkout, you can enter your address to see if we deliver to your location.

How much does shipping cost?

Shipping costs vary based on your location and order value:

  • Domestic: $4.99 for orders under $50, free for orders over $50
  • International: Calculated at checkout based on destination and package weight
  • Express Shipping: Flat rate of $19.99 for 2-3 day delivery
Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all domestic orders over $50. For international orders, free shipping is available on orders over $150. Some promotional items may have different shipping terms.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary based on your location and shipping method:

  • Standard Domestic: 3-7 business days
  • Express Domestic: 2-3 business days
  • International Standard: 7-14 business days
  • International Express: 3-5 business days

Note: These are estimates and may be affected by customs processing in your country.

Can I track my order?

Yes, all orders include tracking. Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with your tracking number and a link to monitor your package's progress. You can also view tracking information in your account dashboard.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 100 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. During checkout, you'll see the exact cost for your location.

What courier services do you use?

We partner with several reliable carriers depending on your location:

  • Domestic: UPS, FedEx, USPS
  • International: DHL, FedEx International
  • Specialty Items: Specialized carriers for fragile or high-value items
Can I change my shipping address after ordering?

You can change your shipping address within 2 hours of placing your order by contacting customer service. After that, changes may not be possible as your order enters our fulfillment system. If your order has shipped, we cannot reroute packages.

What if my package is delayed?

While we make every effort to deliver on time, occasional delays can occur due to weather, customs, or carrier issues. If your package is significantly delayed:

  1. Check the tracking information for updates
  2. Contact the carrier directly with your tracking number
  3. If unresolved, contact our support team for assistance
What if my package is lost or stolen?

If your tracking shows delivered but you haven't received your package:

  1. Check with neighbors or building management
  2. Contact the carrier to verify delivery details
  3. If confirmed lost/stolen, contact us within 7 days

We'll work with the carrier to resolve the issue and arrange a replacement or refund.

Returns & Exchanges

What's your return policy?

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee on all regular-priced items. Items must be:

  • Unworn and unwashed
  • In original condition with tags attached
  • Accompanied by proof of purchase

Final sale items are not eligible for return or exchange.

How do I initiate a return?

To initiate a return:

  1. Log in to your account and visit the Orders section
  2. Select the item(s) you wish to return
  3. Print the prepaid return label
  4. Package your items securely and attach the label
  5. Drop off at any carrier location

Guest customers can start returns using the order number and email address.

Can I exchange an item instead of returning it?

Yes, we offer free exchanges for size or color within 30 days. To exchange an item:

  1. Initiate a return as described above
  2. Select "Exchange" instead of "Return"
  3. Choose the replacement item you want
  4. We'll ship your new item once we receive your return
How long do returns take to process?

Once we receive your return:

  • Processing takes 3-5 business days
  • Refunds are issued within 10 business days
  • Exchanges ship within 2 business days of processing

You'll receive email notifications at each step of the process.

Do I have to pay for return shipping?

We provide free return shipping for domestic returns. International customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to our error (wrong item or defective product).
