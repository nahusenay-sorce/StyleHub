About E-Hebesha

Your trusted partner in cutting-edge technology solutions

Our Mission

At E-Hebesha, we're committed to bringing the latest technology to Ethiopia at affordable prices. We believe everyone deserves access to high-quality electronics that enhance daily life and boost productivity.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between global tech innovations and local consumers through our carefully curated selection of products.

Our Vision

We envision becoming Ethiopia's most trusted tech marketplace, known for our authentic products, exceptional service, and community-focused approach.

By 2030, we aim to expand our network across East Africa, making cutting-edge technology accessible to millions while supporting local tech ecosystems.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We operate with honesty and transparency, ensuring all our products are 100% genuine with proper warranties.

Customer Focus

Your satisfaction is our priority. We offer personalized support and after-sales service that goes above and beyond.

Innovation

We continuously seek the latest technologies to keep our customers at the forefront of digital advancement.
